Piecewise Functions

Preamble:

\usepackage{amsmath}

LaTeX:

\[
    f(x) = \begin{cases}
        \frac{1}{2} \text{ $x$ is heads} \\
        \frac{1}{2} \text{ $x$ is tails}
    \end{cases}
\]

Output: \[ f(x) = \begin{cases} \frac{1}{2} \text{ $x$ is heads} \\ \frac{1}{2} \text{ $x$ is tails} \end{cases} \]
